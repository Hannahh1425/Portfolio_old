The first time I heard the term “Human-centered design”, I fall in love with it. I used to think that design is all about creating things out of the blue and drawing perfectly.

However, after two years’ study of cognitive science and design process, I’m happy to find out that the essential of design is PEOPLE. The priority of design is not aesthetic, but to meet people’s need.

Understanding people and helping them reach their goals is the career that I want to pursue for my whole lifetime. Design, in my opinion, is a unique way to meet people’s need. I enjoy it so much.
